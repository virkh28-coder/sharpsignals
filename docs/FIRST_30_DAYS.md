# First 30 Days Content Calendar

## Principle

**Trust-building before monetization push.** First 30 days = establish methodology, build public track record, educate audience. Paid upsell only from day 25+.

## Daily rhythm

- **9:00 AM ET** — Automated pick drops for the day (1–3 picks across sports in season)
- **12:00 PM ET** — Educational Reel (60s explainer on a betting concept)
- **6:00 PM ET** — Public track-record update (yesterday's results + running CLV)

## Content ratios

- **70% value** (picks, education, transparency)
- **20% social proof** (track record, testimonials when they arrive, behind-the-scenes)
- **10% CTA** (newsletter, affiliate, community)

## Every post includes

- 18+ disclaimer
- "Not betting advice — analytical content for entertainment. ConnexOntario 1-866-531-2600."
- Link in bio → landing page with Google Sheet track record + newsletter signup + affiliate

## What to NOT post, ever

- ❌ "Lock of the day"
- ❌ "Guaranteed winner"
- ❌ "VIP members eating 🔥🔥🔥"
- ❌ Screenshots of single winning slips without also showing losses
- ❌ Fake Venmo/CashApp "subscriber winnings" screenshots
- ❌ Comparisons to other tipsters by name
- ❌ Anything implying inside/fixed/rigged info
